Any organisation is only as good as the quality of the members that make up its structure. Even The Belong Foundation is no exception. Our backbone is the network of volunteers we have managed to build in a short span of time. Understandably, our progress depends on how many more such like-minded individuals come together to work for the cause. Volunteers are required for a wide range of support verticals, such as for teaching, fund raising and other back-end support systems. Donate

The core value with which Belong Foundation function is to provide the best of social jounrey to the key stake holders of the system
our children, the volunteers and supporters.It is these donations that help us meet the monetary requirement for the smooth functioning of the the organizations.

  • Support a Child

    Your donation will be made towards the education of one child. Either the donation can be made for all his educational, domestic and social needs, or for each independently. As a donor you get the privilege of personal mentoring the child and monitoring his progress.

  • Support a Programme

    Your donation will be channelled towards the smooth conduct of a specialised workshop or activity. It could be for a visit to a museum, to a factory or even an educational camp. Alternatively, those interested can help connect The Belong Foundation with institutions and persons of repute from chosen fields for providing educational support.

  • Support a Community

    Your donation will be used to improve basic living conditions, food habits, school facilities or any other socially relevant development changes for the community involved. Donors are invited to personally become part of such initiatives and ensure that their expertise and finances are utilised in a rightful manner.

  • Support for Belong

    Your donation will be used for strengthening the skill sets of our volunteers, maintaining basic accounts and paying bills, as well as to finance the travel allowances of our volunteers.
